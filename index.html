3. Impact of Credit Access on Food Security Among Women Rice Farmers in Ekiti State

The analysis utilized probit regression models and food security indices to determine the factors contributing to food security. Results indicated that access to credit significantly improves food security, allowing farmers to invest in better inputs, while factors such as large household sizes had a negative impact.

Tools used: SAS for regression analysis, Excel for data management Statistical methods: Probit Regression, Food Security Index, Budgetary Analysis.
